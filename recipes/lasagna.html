Easy Sheet Pan Chicken & Vegetables

Description

This sheet pan chicken dinner is a perfect solution for busy weeknights. Juicy chicken and colorful vegetables bake together in one pan, absorbing all the flavors while you relax. Minimal prep, minimal cleanup, maximum taste.

  • salt 2 tsp
  • chicken thighs 1 kg
  • black pepper 1 pinch
  • paprika 1 tsp
  • potatoes 4 pcs
  • bell pepper 2 pcs
  • olive oil 3 tbsp

Steps

  1. Season the chicken with salt, pepper and paprika.
  2. Peel and cut the potatoes into wedges. Slice the bell peppers.
  3. Place chicken and vegetables on a baking tray. Drizzle with olive oil and mix well.
  4. Bake in a preheated oven at 200°C for about 40–45 minutes, until golden and cooked through.
